How we Work

STAGE

01

  1. Initial measurement at site by our expert.
  2. Preliminary design discussion at showroom/site.
  3. Finilize the design & costing details.
  4. Confirmation of design with 50% 0f total amount in advance.
  5. Production starts with tentative delivery date given.

STAGE

02

  1. Electrical ,Plumbing, Chimney hole & gas connection markings.
  2. Flooring , walls to be plastered & tiled by the client.
  3. Final measurement when all civil work is done.

STAGE

03

  1. Find inspection of kitchen modules at showroom and 40% of total amount payment.
  2. Dispatch of kitchen & installation starts.
  3. Balance 10% payment after installation.
